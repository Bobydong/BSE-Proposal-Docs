PALANTIR

Creating a direct pipeline between UCLA's most driven engineering students and one of the most consequential technology companies in the world.

BSE seeks to establish a founding-year partnership with Palantir to create a direct pipeline between UCLA's most driven engineering students and industry leaders like Palantir. There is a plethora of talent at UCLA, but the rapidly changing landscape makes it unclear what skills to prioritize. We want Palantir to work with us to define what the ideal candidate looks like — and collaborate to host events (fireside chats, webinars, tech talks) that bring Palantir's engineering culture directly to our 250+ members. In return, BSE will build a curriculum tailored to Palantir's exact needs, and deliver live on-campus interviews with UCLA's top Bruin talent — screened, tested, and filtered to the best ~10 candidates ready to interview.

We Ask of Palantir

  • Tell us what you seek — work with BSE to define the skills and profile of your ideal candidate
  • Collaborate on events — co-host fireside chats, webinars, and tech talks that bring Palantir's engineering culture to campus
  • Engage our community — connect Palantir engineers with BSE's mentorship and project tracks

What Palantir Gets

  • Curriculum built around you — an extensive, in-demand skills track co-designed with Palantir's input
  • On-Campus Interview Day — we screen, test, and filter UCLA's talent pool, delivering ~10 of the best candidates directly to you
  • Brand visibility across BSE's website, event signage, newsletters, and social media

Why Palantir + BSE

BSE members are builders trained to move beyond coursework — they deploy, iterate, and ship. Palantir builds software for the world's hardest problems, demanding engineers with exactly that mindset. By partnering with BSE, Palantir gains early access to students shaped by a curriculum built to Palantir's own standards, mentored by industry engineers, and ready to contribute from day one. We don't just send résumés — we send prepared engineers.
